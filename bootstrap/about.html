Our Mission

To keep people cozy with premium coats created from sustainable materials and responsible manufacturing, blending comfort with effortless style.

Quality & Craft

We partner with artisans and factories that meet strict quality standards — every seam, lining, and finish is inspected to ensure longevity.

Ethical Sourcing

We prioritize cruelty-free materials (high-quality faux fur and recycled fabrics) and transparent supply chains.

Our Story

Founded by two friends who wanted winter to feel less like a chore and more like a cozy ritual, CozyCoats began as a small collection of thoughtfully designed outerwear. Today we serve customers who value warmth, comfort, and conscious choices.

  • • Launched first collection in a small studio
  • • Switched to 100% faux-fur and recycled linings in 2019
  • • Expanded to international shipping and local partnerships
